We're pleased to announce the release of the latest DYNAMO Magazine! 

body: >-


Titled PROCESS, PRACTICE, PERSPECTIVE, the publication dedicated to in-depth exploration of artistic process and practice within contemporary circus. Among the contributors in the issue, I can name circus artist and professor Harm van der Laan, artists Laura Murphy, Wes Peden, choreographer Simone Wierød, Circus Dance Festival Director Tim Behren, director and choreographer Kitt Johnson, and more.The issue packs original photography, essays, and inspiring interviews in 132 beautifully designed pages, in English, with cover art by German photographer Lily Schlinker, and Swedish artist Fridamy Roth. The magazine can be bought on our website. For bigger orders, write us at magazine@dynamoworkspace.dk
