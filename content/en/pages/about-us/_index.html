Presentation

Circostrada is the European network for contemporary circus and street arts. Created in 2003, its mission is to promote the development, strengthening and recognition of these sectors at European and international level. A key player and point of reference for its members, it is also an essential partner in dialogue with cultural representatives of European institutions.
